New Formats!
Joining our traditional 10 day formats, our new 6 day formats are here! Both Junior and Senior Forum have new 6 day camp sessions to fulfill the goal of attending SuperCamp while still fitting into a busy summer schedule. Participants gain all of the academic, life skills, and personal benefits of our 10 day formats in a streamlined 6 days. These new formats include Quantum Reading, Quantum Strategies, and newly designed Quantum Writing academics along with Outdoor adventure Day and life skills that will help participants excel in school and in life.
