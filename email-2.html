New Formats!
Joining our traditional 10 day formats, our new 6 day formats are here! Both Junior and Senior Forum have new 6 day camp sessions to fulfill the goal of attending SuperCamp while still fitting into a busy summer schedule. Participants gain all of the academic, life skills, and personal benefits of our 10 day formats in a streamlined 6 days. These new formats include Quantum Reading, Quantum Strategies, and newly designed Quantum Writing academics along with Outdoor adventure Day and life skills that will help participants excel in school and in life.

FIT 2017
We love our FIT’s! Talent comes from all over the globe! This year training these dynamic facilitators will take place in 2 locations. In June, our training team will head to Chicago to run our first ever Chicago FIT. In July, Cal State San Marcos will host another group of 2017 FIT’s that are gearing up for a productive summer. We would also like to announce our newest addition to the training program; Year Round FIT’s. These FACs will be gaining additional insight throughout the year at the QLN campus, and lead the way for the 2018 FIT’s for SuperCamp next summer.
