New Formats!
Joining our traditional 10 day formats, our new 6 day formats are here! Both Junior and Senior Forum have new 6 day camp sessions to fulfill the goal of attending SuperCamp while still fitting into a busy summer schedule. Participants gain all of the academic, life skills, and personal benefits of our 10 day formats in a streamlined 6 days. These new formats include Quantum Reading, Quantum Strategies, and newly designed Quantum Writing academics along with Outdoor dventure Day and life skills that will help participants excel in school and in life.

  FIT 2017
We love our FIT’s! Talent comes from all over the globe! This year training these dynamic facilitators will take place in 2 locations. In June, our training team will head to Chicago to run our first ever Chicago FIT. In July, Cal State San Marcos will host another group of 2017 FIT’s that are gearing up for a productive summer. We would also like to announce our newst addition to the training program; Year Round FIT’s. These FACs will be gaining additional insight throughout the year at the QLN campus, and lead the way for the 2018 FIT’s for SuperCamp next summer.

Welcome to The Team!
 
We are happy to announce that Cheyenne Foster and Skyler Foster are now a part of our SuperCamp Team at our headquarters in Oceanside, CA! They are 100% dedicated to making sure we provide the best camp experience possible.
Dr. Cheyenne Foster has been with QLN/SuperCamp for 11 years as a Lead Facilitator, Site Administrator, and Curriculum Developer. Her passion is working with teens and their families to improve the family dynamic, and supporting teachers with curriculum design and classroom management. In her free time Cheyenne loves to spend time outside with her family, work with essential oils, and run half marathons at Disneyland.
